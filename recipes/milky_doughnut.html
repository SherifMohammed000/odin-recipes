INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup powdererd milk for milk filling
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • oil to deep fry
  • 2 tablespoon evaporated milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoon softened butter
  • 1/2 tablespoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup powdered milk
    •

Alright now dusty your pans and lets get cooking

PROCEDURE:

  1. Whisk the yeast, warm water(liquid milk), and sugar.Then let it proof for 5-10 minutes till it's foamy at the top.
  2. Add the flour, powdered milk (if using), eggs,salt,vanilla, and the yeast mixture in a stand mixing bowl.
  3. Mix manually if you dont own a mixer.
  4. Add the softened butter and mix again for 5-7 minutes until the dough is slightly sticky.
  5. Lightly grease a bowl with oil and place the dough inside.Rub some oil on the dough so it dosen't dry or crusty.
  6. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour 30 minutes.
  7. Cut parchment paper into 12 x 12 sizes and place it on the working surface.Sprinkle some flour on it.
  8. Divide the dough into 10-12 portions and roll each portion to form a ball.
  9. Cover with a cloth and let it proof again for 45 minutes.
  10. Add oil to a deep pot or pan over medium heat. You can use a thermometer to reach 375â„‰.
  11. Add 2-3 donuts at a time and fry for 1 minute each side till golden brown. DO NOT OVER CROWD THE PAN.
  12. Remove the donuts and place them on absorbent paper.
  13. Combine the powdered, evaporated and condensed milk and mix well until smooth.
  14. Transfer the mixture into a piping bag.
  15. Using a knife, poke a hole in the side of each doughnut and fill it with the milk filling.

BANANA BREAD RECIPE

The famous odin banana bread recipe is here

Lets dive right in;

INGREDIENTS:

  • ripe bananas
  • flour
  • cane sugar
  • melted butter
  • eggs
  • baking soda
  • walnuts
  • sea salt
  • cinnamon,nutmeg and vanilla

Now lets get to baking

  1. First,Mash the bananas with a potato masher or fork, and place them in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add the sugar, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla and whisk until well combined.
  3. Next,In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  4. Add the flour mixture to the banana mixture and stir.
  5. Fold the walnuts into the butter.
  6. Transfer the butter to a greased loaf pan and sprinkle more walnuts on top.Bake at 350Â°F for 50 to 60 minutes.
